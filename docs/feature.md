Role: PRODUCT PLANNER & FLOW CONTROLLER

## Real User

Musanze Service Desk Operator
(A staff member who records services provided to clients and calculates totals.)

## Problem Being Solved

Currently, the service desk operator:

Calculates totals manually

May make mistakes in price × quantity

Has no clear digital record of services provided

Cannot easily track past transactions

Solution:
Build a simple service recording system that:

Allows selecting a service

Enters client name

Inputs quantity


## Fields Used by ALL Services

These are mandatory fields in the system:

client (text input)

service (dropdown selection)

quantity (number input)

price (auto-filled or manual input)

total (quantity × price, auto-calculated)

Formula:

total = quantity × price

The system must display:

## Live Total Preview

"Record saved successfully" after submission
